About Examinations Department

The Examinations Department is one of the most important pillars of the University. The Examinations Department of the FUUAST is dedicated to ensure the smooth and efficient administration of all academic assessments, conduct of examinations, issuance of results, semester result cards, transcripts, provisional certificates, degrees, preparation of convocation activities etc.

The Examinations department of the FUUAST is headed by the Controller of Examinations, and is responsible for all allied matters of Examinations. The University has running two semesters in a calendar year and the duration of each semester is 18 weeks.

📌 Important Requirements

  • 75% attendance is mandatory to appear in Final-term examinations
  • The department maintains strict rules and regulations for versatile student development
  • Two semesters run annually with 18-week duration each
  • All promotional and grading criteria must be met for progression

🎓 Promotional Criteria for BS Programmes (except Electrical Engineering)

Effective from Spring 2012

  • 1st to 2nd Semester: No criterion for promotion
  • 2nd to 3rd Semester: 80% courses must be passed along with minimum 1.50 CGPA
  • 3rd to 4th Semester: Minimum 1.80 CGPA is required
  • 4th to 5th Semester: 100% courses of 1st and 2nd semester + 80% of 3rd and 4th semester courses must be passed along with 2.00 CGPA
  • 5th to 6th Semester: Minimum 2.20 CGPA is required
  • 6th to 7th Semester: 100% courses of 1st to 4th semester + 80% courses of 5th and 6th semester must be passed along with minimum 2.20 CGPA
  • 7th to 8th Semester: Minimum 2.20 CGPA is required
  • Degree Qualification: All courses must be passed along with 2.50 CGPA

⚡ Promotional Criteria for B.Sc. Electrical Engineering

Effective from Autumn 2016

Academic Probation Policy:

A student who fails to obtain a minimum GPA of 1.5 at the end of 1st semester shall be placed on academic probation for the 2nd semester being academically deficient.

Academic Dismissal:

In case the student fails to improve CGPA to 2.00 at the end of 2nd semester, their name shall be removed from the Rolls of the University. Students dismissed on academic grounds shall receive an official transcript indicating courses completed along with grades earned.

⚠️ No special approval from competent authority should be entertained to cancel out the academic dismissal.
